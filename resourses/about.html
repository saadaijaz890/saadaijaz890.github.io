About CalmPaws 🐾

We're dog owners who got tired of vague advice and products that didn't work. So we built the resource we wished existed.

Our Mission

CalmPaws exists for one reason: to help anxious dogs feel safe. We research every calming product, read the veterinary studies, and test combinations so that you — a dog owner at your wit's end at 2am — can find a real answer fast.

We're not vets. But every article on this site is reviewed by veterinary professionals before it goes live, and we never recommend a product we wouldn't use ourselves.
