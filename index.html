Quick Answers

The Questions Every Anxious Dog Owner Asks

How long do calming chews take to work?
Most calming chews take 30–90 minutes to work. For best results, give them 45–60 minutes before a triggering event. Zylkene and VetriScience Composure are among the fastest-acting options. See full chews guide →
Do Pugs need anxiety vests?
Yes — Pugs are highly prone to anxiety due to their sensitivity to owner absence. A snug anxiety vest like Thundershirt can reduce stress signals in up to 80% of dogs. Size Small fits most adult Pugs.
Are calming chews or sprays better for separation anxiety?
Use both: calming chews 45 min before leaving, Adaptil spray on their crate for immediate effect. Combined, they address both chemical and environmental triggers. Full separation anxiety protocol →
Best calming product for thunderstorm-scared dogs?
The most effective combo: Thundershirt (pressure therapy) + Adaptil spray on bandana + closing curtains to block lightning. Start 30–45 min before the storm if you have advance warning. Read Thundershirt review →
Can senior dogs use the same calming products?
Mostly yes, but with care. Avoid melatonin in dogs with hormonal conditions. Zylkene and Adaptil are safe for all ages. Always consult your vet for dogs over 10 years old. Senior dog anxiety guide →
Why is my dog pacing at night?
Nighttime pacing is often caused by cognitive dysfunction (especially seniors), pain, or generalized anxiety. A DAP diffuser + melatonin (0.1mg/kg) 30 min before bed is a common starting protocol. See your vet if it persists. Nighttime anxiety guide →
